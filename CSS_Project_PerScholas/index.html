Chocolate Fudge

Description

This is a delicious recipe for chocolate fudge. It's easy to make and perfect for any occasion.

Follow the steps below to create your own chocolately goodness.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of cocoa powder
  • 1/4 cup of milk
  • 2 tablespoons of butter

Steps

  1. Mix sugar and cocoa powder in a saucepan.
  2. Add milk and bring to a boil.
  3. Remove from heat and stir in butter.
  4. Pour into a greased pan and let it cool.
